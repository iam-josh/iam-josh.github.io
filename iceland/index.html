Travel tips

Iceland is absolutely an all year round destination.

If the weather conditions are suitable, hire a car! This is without a doubt the best way to explore the country because you can plan your own schedule and stop off at as many places as you like.

However, if you can’t and/or don’t want to hire a car then there are plenty of tour companies to choose from. Ensure you do your research before booking as prices, group sizes and itineraries all vary!

There are many sights that can be seen on route but the Golden Circle is typically noted for its 3 marvellous main attractions: the Gullfoss waterfall, Geysir Geothermal Area and Ãžingvellir National Park. This particular geothermal area contains the Strokkur Geyser which erupts to heights of 30m every 10-20 minutes. Geysir is one of the world’s most famous geysers and has given its name to other hot springs across the world. Nowadays it’s mostly dormant and only erupts on the odd occasion, usually when there is an earthquake.
